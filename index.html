About

Alanna Koons is an artist and graphic designer. Residing in the Indianapolis area, she specializes in branding and messaging for non-profit organizations.

Alanna graduated with her B.F.A. from Brigham Young University in 2014. As a student she interned at several art museums and learned from travelling artists who greatly influenced her design work.

Since graduating college, Alanna has worked as a marketing specialist for the Indianapolis chapter of Volunteers of America, as well as doing consulting work with the Indianapolis Parksinsons Foundation and several other small businesses.

Alanna also loves animals!
